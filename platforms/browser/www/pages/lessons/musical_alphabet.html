Musical Alphabet
Natural Notes

Thera are only 7 natural notes witch are represented by seven first letters of the alphabet.

A B C D E F G

Distance between each note is called Half-Step or Whole-Step. Half-Step is equivalent of 1 fret difference on guitar fingerboard. Whole-Step is equivalent of two frets.

Half-Step = 1 Fret
Whole Step = 2 Frets

In natural notes only between B-C and E-F you will find Half-Step. All other notes have Whole-Step between them.
